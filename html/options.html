Specific video and channel options

To add a custom blacklist item, click the plus. You can filter out any video that has a specific phrase in the title by choosing the "Any Channel Contains" channel filter option.
You can filter out videos from some a specific channel by choosing the "does contain" or "does not contain" channel filter option.

  • "Does Contain": This option will filter out any video from the selected channel that contains the configured text.
  • "Does Not Contain": This option will filter out all videos from a channel that do not contain the configured text.
